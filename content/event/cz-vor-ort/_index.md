What does the Kaiserslautern university location have to offer? On Monday, four researchers funded by the Carl Zeiss Foundation (Carl-Zeiss-Stiftung) showcased exactly this at the *CZS vor Ort* event. For the sixth time, the foundation invited grantees, alumni, and friends of the foundation to one of its supported university locations. This time, attendees met at *42kaiserslautern*. CZS Managing Director Dr. Felix Streiter moderated a short panel discussion with the President of the RPTU and the Vice President for Research and Transfer of the Kaiserslautern University of Applied Sciences.

Following this, the funded scientists presented exemplar insights from their research projects. In addition, the foundation outlined its ongoing funding strategies and available opportunities. Over 60 guests accepted the invitation, actively exchanging ideas with representatives of the Carl-Zeiss-Stiftung in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere.
